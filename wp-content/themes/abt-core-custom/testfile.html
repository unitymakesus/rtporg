Innovative Space

We are offering private office space to pioneers as a place to come set up shop and get working. Repurposing an older building allows us to be flexible and affordable by lowering the cost of work space and thus lowering the barriers to success and encouraging innovation. We are accepting applications for pioneers who wish to enrich education, create meaningful work and serve others. Pioneers who will leave what is known and pursue what lies ahead – the Frontier. Please fill out the application online describing why you desire space at the Frontier – why you want to be a pioneer.

Interested entrepreneurs, artists and dreamers will follow the link to contact The Frontier staff for information about availabilitys. This is a rolling application process and decisions regarding applicants will be made in the order they are received. Listed below are the amenities included in the monthly membership structure. We look forward to reviewing your application to explore the future that awaits.

Pioneer Membership – $300 per month (on a flexible month-to-month basis):

  • Office on 3rd floor with 24/7 access
  • Access to meeting and conference room space (must reserve in advance)
  • Free Wi-Fi in common areas and wired Internet to each office
  • Access to kitchen and complimentary coffee
  • Access to RTP events and programs – Ex: Fidelity Food Truck Rodeos every Friday,
    RTP 180, 1 Million Cups, DISRUPT
  • Listing on RTP website
  • Access to 1st floor community space – the “living room”
  • Bike racks
  • Furniture Package for Office (L-shaped desk, office chair, and two guest chairs)
